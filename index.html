Na Power! z Akešem

After the summer hit that was Pokemon Go, the geolocation service for apps and games became popular. In this wake, the client wanted to do something similar. An RPG geolocation web app was crated, with upgradable skills that directly tied to one's own real life strengths, such as financial responsibility, confidence and more. The app had a radar, and when you pussed the scan button, it was updated - at the same time, you dropped a signal at your current location, and you are were able to see all signals dropped by other nearby players. With interacting with their signal, you could improve your own skills, if they were more experienced in it.

Technologies used

HTML5 CSS3 Javascript PHP MySQL AJAX jQuery SASS Google Maps API Grunt
